CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY MANUAL
Version 4.2 | Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Approved by: VP Customer Experience

=======================================================
1. REFUND AND RETURN POLICY
=======================================================

1.1 Physical Products
  - 30-day return window from delivery date
  - Item must be unused and in original packaging
  - Customer pays return shipping unless product is defective

1.2 Digital Products / Subscriptions
  - 30-day refund window for unused digital products
  - Partial month subscriptions refunded on a pro-rata basis
  - Annual subscription refunds available within first 30 days only

1.3 Service Credits
  - Issued automatically for outages exceeding 99.5% uptime SLA
  - Credits expire 12 months from issue date
  - Non-transferable; cannot be converted to cash

=======================================================
2. COMPLAINT ESCALATION PROCEDURE
=======================================================

Level 1 – Tier 1 Support
  - First point of contact via email, chat, or phone
  - Resolution target: within 24 hours
  - Authority: apply account credit up to $50, process standard refunds

Level 2 – Tier 2 Support (Escalation from Tier 1)
  - Triggered when Tier 1 cannot resolve within 48 hours
  - OR when customer requests escalation
  - Resolution target: within 48 hours of escalation
  - Authority: apply credit up to $200, override standard policies

Level 3 – Manager Review
  - Triggered when Tier 2 cannot resolve within 5 business days
  - OR for complaints involving legal/compliance risk
  - Resolution target: within 5 business days of escalation
  - Authority: full discretion including contract modifications

Customer Notification:
  Customers are notified via email AND in-app notification
  at each escalation stage. Failure to notify within 2 hours
  of escalation is a policy violation.

=======================================================
3. SLA BY COMPLAINT TYPE
=======================================================

Critical Billing Complaints   : Resolution within 4 business hours
  (affecting access to paid services or duplicate charges > $100)

Standard Billing Complaints   : Resolution within 1 business day

Technical Outage Complaints   : Acknowledgement within 15 minutes,
                                 resolution within 4 hours

Product / Quality Complaints  : Resolution within 3 business days

General Feedback              : Acknowledgement within 24 hours,
                                 no resolution SLA

=======================================================
4. DATA PRIVACY & STORAGE POLICY
=======================================================

Governing regulations:
  - EU customers             : GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  - California customers     : CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  - All other regions        : Internal Data Governance Policy v3.1

Complaint data retention:
  - Active complaint records : retained for duration of case + 24 months
  - Archived complaint data  : retained for 7 years for compliance
  - Audio recordings         : retained for 90 days, then deleted

Customer rights:
  - Right to access own complaint history (GDPR Art. 15)
  - Right to erasure of personal complaint data after retention period
  - Opt-out of complaint data analytics (CCPA)

=======================================================
5. QUALITY ASSURANCE
=======================================================

Monthly KPI targets for Tier 1:
  - FCR Rate                  : ≥ 72%
  - CSAT Score                : ≥ 4.2
  - Average Handle Time       : ≤ 7 minutes
  - 24-hr Resolution Rate     : ≥ 80%

Agent performance reviews: Quarterly
Coaching threshold: Agents scoring below target for 2 consecutive months
  receive mandatory coaching sessions.

=======================================================
6. AGENT CONDUCT POLICY
=======================================================

Agents must:
  - Greet customers by name
  - Not place customer on hold for more than 3 minutes without check-in
  - Document all interactions in the CRM within 15 minutes of close
  - Never promise outcomes outside their authority level

Zero-tolerance violations:
  - Sharing customer PII without authorisation
  - Falsifying complaint records
  - Discriminatory conduct toward any customer
