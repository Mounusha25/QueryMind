PRODUCT IMPROVEMENT RECOMMENDATIONS
Q3 2025 Analysis — Engineering & Product Prioritisation Brief
Prepared by: Customer Experience Analytics Team
Date: October 10, 2025

=======================================================
OVERVIEW
=======================================================

This document synthesises Q3 complaint data, NPS feedback,
and customer feature requests into prioritised product
improvement recommendations for Q4 2025 and H1 2026.

Total unique feature requests logged in Q3: 142
Customer feedback responses analysed: 8,914
Complaint-to-feature-request linkage coverage: 76%

=======================================================
TOP 3 PRODUCT IMPROVEMENTS REQUESTED BY CUSTOMERS
=======================================================

1. FASTER CHECKOUT EXPERIENCE
   Requested by: 3,412 unique customers (38% of app complainants)
   NPS impact potential: +14 points
   Projected conversion rate impact: +8% to +12%
   Effort estimate: 6-8 sprints (engineering)
   Priority: P0

2. IMPROVED MOBILE UI / UX
   Requested by: 2,891 unique customers
   Includes: intuitive navigation, responsive layouts,
   accessibility improvements (WCAG 2.1 AA compliance)
   Projected complaint reduction: ~35% of mobile category
   Effort estimate: 4-6 sprints
   Priority: P0

3. TRANSPARENT BILLING & INVOICE CLARITY
   Requested by: 2,577 unique customers
   Includes: itemised invoices, real-time billing alerts,
   clear subscription change confirmations
   Projected billing complaint reduction: 40%
   Effort estimate: 3-4 sprints
   Priority: P1

=======================================================
HIGHEST ROI IMPROVEMENT
=======================================================

AUTOMATED BILLING RECONCILIATION
  Description: Replace manual CSV-based reconciliation with
  an automated pipeline that detects and resolves discrepancies
  in real time before charges post.

  Projected outcomes:
    - 65% reduction in billing dispute volume
    - $1.1M annual savings in complaint handling costs
    - 18% reduction in overall support operational cost
    - Estimated payback period: 4 months post-launch

  This was identified as the single highest-ROI engineering
  investment for Q4 2025.

=======================================================
ENGINEERING PRIORITISATION FOR Q4 2025
  (To reduce complaint volume by ≥ 15%)
=======================================================

Rank  Area                          Complaint %  Effort    Impact
  1   Automated billing reconcil.     28%         Medium    Very High
  2   Mobile checkout redesign        24%         High      High
  3   Shipping tracking integration   19%         Low       Medium-High

Combined, these three initiatives address 71% of total complaint
volume. Achieving even 50% reduction in each area yields a
projected overall complaint reduction of ~35%, exceeding the 15%
target by a significant margin.

=======================================================
ADDITIONAL RECOMMENDED INITIATIVES
=======================================================

4. LOGISTICS PARTNER SLA ENFORCEMENT
   - Renegotiate carrier contracts to enforce 95% on-time rate
   - Implement automated carrier performance dashboards
   - Introduce secondary carrier routing for at-risk shipments
   - Projected shipping complaint reduction: 40–50%

5. SELF-SERVICE BILLING PORTAL
   - Allow customers to view, dispute, and resolve billing issues
     without contacting support
   - Estimated deflection of 22% of all Tier 1 billing tickets
   - Aligns with customer preference for self-service (58% prefer
     self-service for billing inquiries per Q3 survey)

6. ENTERPRISE ACCOUNT HEALTH DASHBOARD
   - Dedicated dashboard for enterprise accounts showing usage,
     billing history, open complaints, and SLA performance
   - Addresses the disproportionate complaint rate from enterprise
     segment (2.1 complaints per 100 orders vs 0.9 for consumers)
